We are Thompson and Thompson Consulting.

Strategies and solutions for energy sector companies

We are a family owned boutique consulting firm working with the federal, state, and local government & communities across America to ensure the energy transition is a success.

Our team's success relies upon three pillars. One, life-long learners. Our team is always learning "what's new" and "what works". Two, we work to make things work. Three, we undestand how to create solutions that generate advantages within your industry.

WHAT WE DO

We offer professional services in these four areas.

Energy transition technologies face challenges in human factors, potentially reducing system performance and leading to suboptimal user experiences. We apply proven human factors methods, such as task analysis and performance assessments, to ensure that energy systems are efficient, user-friendly, and ready for real-world applications. Our insights help drive the design and development of energy technologies that seamlessly integrate with human needs.

Developing and maintaining digital platforms for energy companies can be complex and time-consuming. Our web development team provides tailored solutions to ensure energy firms have a strong online presence. From showcasing innovative energy solutions to engaging customers, we deliver platforms that align with your business goals, all while offering a cost-effective and streamlined process.

Optimizing the digital customer experience for energy solutions is crucial for enhancing engagement and driving conversions. A well-optimized user experience can increase digital adoption rates and system efficiency. Our UX experts will assess and redesign your energy-related platforms, identifying and fixing issues, improving customer satisfaction, and increasing retention.

Energy companies often require complex backend systems to manage large-scale data from smart grids, renewable energy sources, and IoT devices. Our programming team specializes in developing and optimizing such systems using Python, Java, and MySQL, ensuring they meet industry standards and deliver reliable, efficient performance.
