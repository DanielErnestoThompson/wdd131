Website Plan for Thompson and Thompson Consulting

This name represents the consulting firm founded by Daniel Thompson, focusing on corporate strategy, innovation, and solutions to navigate the energy transition. The name reflects a professional and trustworthy brand.

Scenarios

  • **What services does Thompson and Thompson Consulting offer?**

    The site will provide a detailed list of consulting services, ranging from strategy development to operational optimization, focusing on sectors like energy, digital transformation, and human capital management.

    There will be a contact page with a form and direct contact information (phone, email, etc.) for prospective clients.

Color Schema

Primary Color: **Dark Blue (#000080)** for the website's headers, navigation bar, and footer to give a professional and sleek appearance.

Secondary Color: **White (#FFFFFF)** for the background, ensuring good readability and contrast.

Accent Color: **Light Gray (#D3D3D3)** for sections like testimonials and secondary headers, providing subtle contrast.

Typography

Heading Font: **Montserrat (sans-serif)** for all headings (h1, h2, etc.) to give a modern and bold look to the website.

Body Font: **Arial (sans-serif)** for paragraphs and body text, ensuring easy readability across devices.

Special Font: **Merriweather (serif)** for quotes or testimonials to add a touch of elegance.
